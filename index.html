Welcome Message

FSS Oyo: We Pioneer Geoinformatics Education in Nigeria

I welcome you to Federal School of Surveying, Oyo — the beacon of Surveying and Geoinformatics education in Nigeria and Sub-Saharan Africa. Here, professionals and technologists in the field of Land Surveying, Geoinformatics, and other related programmes are trained for the development of the National Economy.

As a pioneer in Surveying Education and a Centre of Excellence, our aim is to strive at producing graduates who shall be sound and proficient in the practice of the profession.

You are welcome to explore our website for adequate information on the activities in the School.

Who We Are

Our History

Established as the first of its kind, the Federal School of Surveying, Oyo has a rich history of academic excellence and professional training.

Our Mission

To produce Professionals, Technologists, and Technicians in the Field of Surveying and Geoinformatics for the National Economy in a sustainable and conducive learning environment.

Our Vision

To be a pioneer in Surveying Education and Centre of Excellence, producing graduates who are sound, technically confident, and maintain high ethical standards.
